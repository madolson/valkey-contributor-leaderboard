Methodology

This leaderboard tracks contributions across all valkey-io repositories since the Redis→Valkey fork (March 28, 2024).

  • Commits — only mainline commits with a merged PR that received at least one approval from a reviewer other than the author.
  • Reviews — each approved PR review counts as one point for the reviewer.
  • Score — commits + reviews.

Bot accounts are excluded. Click a contributor's name to see their full commit and review history.
